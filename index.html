L'Oréal invests heavily in scientific research and innovation, allowing it to continually refresh its product lines. The brand is also committed to sustainability and social initiatives.

Estée Lauder

Founded in 1946 by Estée Lauder and her husband Joseph, Estée Lauder became famous for its high-quality cosmetics and fragrances. One of the brand's major achievements was creating the "try before you buy" concept, which allowed many women to test products before purchasing. Estée Lauder is also renowned for its skincare products, such as serums and creams.

MAC Cosmetics

MAC Cosmetics was established in 1984 in Toronto, Canada. The brand became popular for its professional-grade makeup used by both everyday consumers and makeup artists. MAC is known for its wide range of shades and textures, allowing everyone to find their perfect product. The brand actively promotes inclusivity and diversity, offering cosmetics for all skin tones.
